Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if a unit is tipped over by wind or vandalism?

While our units are designed for stability, severe weather or unforeseen events can happen. Please contact us immediately if a unit is tipped over. We will dispatch a team as soon as possible to assess the situation, clean the area, and replace the unit if necessary. We also offer an optional damage waiver for added peace of mind.

Can I get a same-day or next-day delivery?

We always do our best to accommodate last-minute requests. Depending on our schedule and inventory, we can often provide next-day and sometimes even same-day delivery, especially for standard units. For urgent needs, please call our office directly for the fastest response.

Is a special vehicle needed to move the trailer-mounted portable toilet?

Not at all. The trailer is lightweight and comes with a standard ball hitch, making it easily towable by any standard pickup truck or work van. It's designed for maximum mobility and convenience for your crew.

Can the solar-powered restroom function at night or on cloudy days?

Absolutely. The solar panel charges an onboard battery during daylight hours. This battery stores enough power to run the efficient LED lighting and ventilation fan through the night and during overcast periods, ensuring consistent performance.

What happens if a unit needs service outside of the scheduled day?

While our customized service schedules are designed to be sufficient, we understand that unexpected high usage can occur. If a unit requires immediate attention, please call our office directly. We can arrange an unscheduled service call to resolve the issue promptly.

Is the water in the sink of a portable toilet safe for handwashing?

Absolutely. The water in the sink is held in a separate, sealed freshwater tank within the unit. It is not connected to the waste tank in any way and is perfectly clean and safe for handwashing.
